Company Profile

If you are looking for hand-crafted solid wood furniture, laminated board, and MS furniture with unique designs for your home or office, FORAGI FURNITURE & INTERIOR LTD. is the name for you. The company has been manufacturing, marketing, and distributing high-quality wooden furniture since 2009. FORAGI FURNITURE & INTERIOR LTD. has been one of the pioneer companies that have taken the local furniture company to global standards.

From intense passion in this industry, Mr. Foragi initiated FORAGI FURNITURE & INTERIOR LTD. with core values like ensuring top-notch quality, sincerity in providing service, commitment to teamwork, professionalism, and integrity. This is recognition of Mr. Foragi's commitment and good practice.

At FORAGI FURNITURE & INTERIOR LTD., we have skilled and dedicated craftsmen creating masterpieces for generations. Each piece of furniture carries a unique signature of Foragi's aesthetics and quality. Employees push themselves to surpass the quality benchmark set by them. Our design department is in constant pursuit of offering attractive furniture to our customers. The research and development team concentrates on making the furniture long-lasting and reduces production waste, thus remaining environment-friendly.

The 6500.00 sqft manufacturing plant is equipped with modern technology and production facilities. We have an automatic wood seasoning and chemical plant. The machining section is fully equipped with European automatic woodworking machinery and is managed by skilled operators. The dust-free controlled environment of the finishing section ensures the pristine finish of every piece of furniture.

The Head Office is located at Mohakhali (DOHS) in the capital city of Dhaka and more in the district metropolitans showcase Foragi's offerings. They also act as a terminal for clients' custom orders. Apart from the furniture, Foragi also offers a wide range of accessories to help you arrange your decor as you like.

FORAGI FURNITURE & INTERIOR LTD. has been given the responsibility to do the furnishing of several respectable projects including ITN Building (BUET), North South University, VERC, SB - TEL Enterprise, Care Bangladesh, Diamond World, and several corporate works like Akiz Group in Bangladesh. After successfully catering to the local market,

We are expanding our offerings into office interiors, designer wooden doors, steel furniture, rustic designs, upholstered and home accessories to provide our clients with diversified options.
